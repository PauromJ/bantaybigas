About BantayBigas

A manually curated directory built for trust in B2B rice sourcing.

What Is BantayBigas?

BantayBigas is a curated and verified B2B rice supplier directory—not a marketplace, not a social network, and not an open listing platform. We exist to help legitimate buyers find legitimate suppliers in the rice industry.

Every supplier listed on BantayBigas has been manually reviewed by our team. We do not allow self-service registration or instant listing. Suppliers must apply, and only those that pass our verification process are added to the directory.

Our Verification Process

  1. Application Submission — A supplier submits their details through our application form.
  2. Document Review — Our team reviews business registration documents, licenses, and trade history.
  3. Background Check — We verify the supplier's physical address, operational history, and industry standing.
  4. Manual Approval — Only after thorough review is a supplier listed in the directory.

Important Disclaimer

BantayBigas is not responsible for fraudulent activity that may occur after a supplier has been verified. Our verification process is based on available information at the time of approval. We strongly recommend that buyers conduct their own due diligence before entering into any business agreement.

Listings are based on information provided by the supplier and publicly available records at the time of approval. Circumstances can change, and verification is not a guarantee of future performance. BantayBigas does not facilitate payments, contracts, or transactions between buyers and suppliers.

Profile updates and changes are handled manually by BantayBigas administration. Suppliers cannot edit their own listings. If you believe a listed supplier is no longer trustworthy, please contact us immediately so we can investigate.
