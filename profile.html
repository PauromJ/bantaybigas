Company Overview

Mayon Rice Mills Corporation is a premier rice milling and distribution company based in the Bicol Region of the Philippines. Operating since 1987, we serve institutional buyers, wholesalers, and food manufacturers across Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao.

Our facility processes over 120,000 metric tons of palay annually, producing high-quality well-milled rice, regular milled rice, and specialty varieties. We maintain strict quality control at every stage—from sourcing to packaging.
